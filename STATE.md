## Key Findings from M02

1. **Source-first authoring works.** Including external academic sources during initial writing (rather than adding during review) pushed source_diversity from 3 (M01) to 4 (M02) across all files. Quality trend: M01 avg 3.8 → M02 avg 4.2.
2. **Table-first structure drives actionability.** Files dominated by tables with specific IDs, dates, and criteria (milestones, assessment) scored 5 on actionability. Files with more prose (pedagogy) scored 4 — appropriate for their domain.
3. **Token estimation is calibrating.** M01 actual was 65% of estimate; M02 actual was 92%. The chars/4 method is reliable when content structure is known in advance.
4. **Single-session pattern continues.** All three completed missions (M00 est 2-3, M01 est 2, M02 est 2) finished in 1 session each. M03 (est 1) may be the first mission where estimate matches actual.
