# Pilot A — Peter's Church, Grand Rapids, MI

> **DRAFT** — This pilot plan has not been reviewed by community representatives. Per Standing Order #7, all community content requires community review before it is considered accurate. `#needs-community-review`

## Overview

Pilot A is the **concentrated, single-site model** for the Context Commons program. Anchored at Peter's Church in Grand Rapids, Michigan, it tests whether the circular knowledge economy — elders providing context, youth building capability, stewards governing the process — can function within a single faith-based community hub. Grand Rapids offers strong existing intergenerational infrastructure, a mid-sized city scale where community relationships are dense and navigable, and a growing technology sector that can supply local mentors.

This pilot launches first (Q3 2026) and its learnings inform Pilot B (Los Angeles, Q4 2026). The two pilots are deliberately contrasting — Grand Rapids tests depth in a concentrated setting; Los Angeles tests breadth in a distributed one. Neither is a template for the other (Standing Order #9).
