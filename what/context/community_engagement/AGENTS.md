## What's Here

The operational model for how the Context Commons engages with pilot communities, establishes and governs partnerships, assesses community readiness, and deploys mentors. These 3 files bridge the theoretical framework (program_design) with practical pilot execution (Phase 2). They are the "how we work with people" layer — partnership lifecycle, readiness criteria, and role definitions.
