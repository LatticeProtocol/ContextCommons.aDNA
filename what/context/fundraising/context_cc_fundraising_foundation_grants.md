# Foundation & Corporate Grant Landscape

> **Deadline volatility**: Foundation cycles vary — some are rolling, some annual. Verify current status before starting any application. Last verified: April 2026.

## Why Foundations Matter for CC

Foundation grants are typically easier to apply for than federal grants (2-3 page LOI vs. 30-page federal narrative), decisions are faster (6-12 weeks vs. 6-9 months), and reporting is lighter. They also offer more flexibility — many accept fiscally sponsored projects and fund general operations, not just narrow program deliverables.

These funders' stated priorities overlap directly with CC's design.

| Funder | Program | Range | Cycle | CC Alignment |

## Application Strategy

1. **Submit 3-4 LOIs to local community foundations first** (Grand Rapids CF, CA CF, Wege, Weingart) — build track record with simpler processes
2. **Apply to 1-2 tech company programs** (Google.org, Spectrum) in parallel
3. **Pursue Omidyar Youth Power Fund** when cohort opens — highest individual foundation alignment
4. **Monitor Humanity AI Coalition** for first grant cycle in 2026 — massive pool, direct CC fit
5. **Build visibility for invitation-only funders** (McGovern, Siegel) through research publications, conference presence, and partnerships with existing grantees
